The Panhandle Women’s Project Inc.

About: The Panhandle Women’s Project (PWP) is a student-founded, student-led, legally registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization serving women, girls, and families across the Florida Panhandle. PWP addresses everyday women’s issues that can become barriers to stability and safety, providing free childcare assistance, transportation support, home-help services, and essential items such as hygiene products, clothing, period supplies, and household necessities. The organization also helps women prepare for job interviews and professional opportunities.

PWP fundraises through community events, donation drives, and partnerships with local organizations, ensuring every dollar and donated item goes directly back into the community. By involving students in planning, fundraising, outreach, and service delivery, PWP creates meaningful opportunities for young people to engage in hands-on community service while supporting women and families in need.
