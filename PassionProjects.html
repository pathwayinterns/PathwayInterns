PIVOT: International Youth-Led Think Tank

About: PIVOT is an international and remote youth-led think tank that empowers young people ages 13–19 to research, write, and advocate for policy solutions that address today’s most pressing issues. Our mission is to ensure youth voices are heard at every level of government—local, national, and global. Through collaborative research cohorts, mentorship from policy professionals, and real-world advocacy opportunities, PIVOT fellows are given the tools to turn their ideas into impact. The fellowship is indefinite, offers opportunities to publish original research, and allows fellows to later step into leadership roles. Our team currently includes over 50 youth across the world.

Project Light Shade

About: ProjectLightShade is a youth-led initiative dedicated to advocating for sun safety and skincare equity, particularly for Black and brown communities. Despite the misconception that melanin offers complete protection from the sun, our communities remain vulnerable to sun damage, skin cancer, and hyperpigmentation. ProjectLightShade seeks to raise awareness, educate, and promote healthier skincare practices while addressing the disparities in the skincare industry. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their skin health and to fight for greater representation and inclusivity in sun safety and skincare.

Gen Z Art Journal: "Unbound"

About: "Unbound" is a digital journal dedicated to showcasing the visual and written work of Gen Z creatives. From poetry and short stories to digital collages and photography, this journal accepts submissions year-round and curates quarterly themed issues. Contributors have their work featured on a professionally designed platform and can be considered for the “Editor’s Spotlight” each season. Open to students ages 13–18 globally.

BioBuddies: Peer STEM Mentorship

About: BioBuddies connects high school students interested in biology, chemistry, and environmental science with college mentors studying in related fields. This free program includes monthly virtual check-ins, collaborative mini-research challenges, and guidance on competition and scholarship applications. BioBuddies promotes inclusion in STEM and encourages interdisciplinary exploration across the sciences. Mentorship runs in 3-month cycles with a chance to renew.

Youth Voices for Justice (YVJ)

About: Youth Voices for Justice is a student-led initiative focused on storytelling and advocacy for social equity. Members write op-eds, conduct interviews, and produce short-form media on issues like education, mental health, racial justice, and youth rights. Each participant is assigned a topic cohort and guided by editorial mentors to craft compelling narratives and calls to action. New cycles begin every January and July.
