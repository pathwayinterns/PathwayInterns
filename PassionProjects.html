PIVOT: International Youth-Led Think Tank

About: PIVOT is an international youth-led think tank that empowers young people ages 13–19 to research, write, and advocate for policy solutions that address today’s most pressing issues. Our mission is to ensure youth voices are heard at every level of government—local, national, and global. Through collaborative research cohorts, mentorship from policy professionals, and real-world advocacy opportunities, PIVOT fellows are given the tools to turn their ideas into impact. The fellowship is indefinite, offers opportunities to publish original research, and allows fellows to later step into leadership roles. Our team currently includes over 50 youth across the world.

Project Light Shade

About: ProjectLightShade is a youth-led initiative dedicated to advocating for sun safety and skincare equity, particularly for Black and brown communities. Despite the misconception that melanin offers complete protection from the sun, our communities remain vulnerable to sun damage, skin cancer, and hyperpigmentation.
